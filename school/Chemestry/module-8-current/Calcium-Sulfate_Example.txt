aquesous solutions lithium sulfate and clacium nitrate are mixed to form the precipitate clacium sulfate. Write the (1) balanced molecular (2) complete ionic, and (3) net ionic equations.

Balanced:
Li₂(SO₄)(aq) + Ca(NO₃)₂(aq)  →   Ca(SO₄)₍s₎ + 2Li(NO₃)(ap)

Complete ionic:
  2Li⁺ +  SO₄²⁻ + Ca²⁺ + NO₃⁻ →  Ca(SO₄)₍s₎ + Li⁺ + NO₃⁻ 


net ionic equation:
   SO₄²⁻ + Ca²⁺  →  Ca(SO₄)₍s₎
