Single replacement/redox reaction 
for the reaction between solid aluminum and a copper (II) chloride solution.

Balancing:
  2Al₍s₎ + 3Cu(Cl)₂₍aq₎  → 3Cu₍s₎ + 2Al(Cl)₂₍aq₎

complete ionic:
  2Al₍s₎ + 3Cu²⁺₍aq₎ + 3Cl₂⁻₍aq₎  → 3Cu₍s₎ + 2Al³⁺ + 2Cl₂⁻₍aq₎


net ionic:
  2Al₍s₎ + 3Cu₍aq₎  →  2Al₍aq₎ + 3Cu₍s₎
